Mr. Dr. Charles "Wiggly Butt" Exavier Rigby

Charlie "Wiggly Butt" Rigby (born Charles Barles) had a humble beginning. Not much is known about Charlie's first few months on this earth. What we do know, is that during this time he developed an extreme aversion to bathtubs, ambulances, and cars of any variety.

Approximately one year after he was born, Charles was found wandering the streets of Sunnyside Washington. It is believed that he was abandoned due to a yet undiagnosed medical condition.

On March 19, 2020, Charlie was adopted by loving parents, Aubrey and Richard Rigby. They provided Charlie with lots of snuggles, love, and a safe enviornment to grow. It was during this time that Charles earned his PHD (pretty hyper dog) in wiggleology. He is the leading scholar in this field, with several world renowned publications on the subject.
